Caring and Learning

Heloísa, or “Helô” to her friends (like “L-O”, with the “o” like “lot”, not “OK”), is an educator, psychology student, and animal person. She is fluent in both Portuguese and English, spending her childhood in the USA and her teenage years in Brazil.

With over a decade of experience in education, she thrives on the connection she builds with her students. Helô teaches English to learners of all ages and co-teaches in blended learning programs. She has worked in a range of settings, from state industrial federations to bilingual schools. Her background in psychology gives her a deeper understanding of the complexity of human behaviour. This has made her a critical, empathetic, and adaptive educator. In line with her caring personality, Helô has a love for all living beings. Her ability to care for animals comes from having always had them in her life, be they dogs, cats, horses, chickens, or parrots. In essence, what defines Heloísa is her desire to contribute to the world.

Heloísa welcomes you to her website and is grateful that you have taken the time to find out more about her. She is open to new ideas and eager to collaborate, especially in education, psychology, and animal care. At the bottom of this page, you can find a contact form if you would like to send her a message. Thank you!
